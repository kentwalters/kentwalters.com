Kent Walters

I work on Product & Design at Retool in San Francisco.

Some things I've made:

  • Canadian System of Soil Science texture triangle visualizer. I used to study soils and I've always liked the relative representation of sand, silt, and clay.
  • Slightly more robust version of the physics engine that runs on this site. One of the last things I coded by hand and worked out the math; now it'd be a 2-minute vibe code.
  • Super simple grid visualizer to play with pixel fonts.
  • Figma plugin to generate Retool themes from color variables.
  • Presentation I used to give about designing in Retool. Kind of useless without the speaker notes, but you may enjoy the screenshots of Retool apps that don't look horrible. I've also done a webinar on the topic, and built retool.studio to showcase the ideas in practice.
  • Exploration of how we might represent work being done by many agents communicating with each other and other systems of record, which I made while building Retool Agents.

Mostly I'm interested in human/computer interfaces, which AI is making interesting again (and which I don't believe are going anywhere even with all these agents).
